Jönköping City

Jönköping is a city on the shores of Lake Vättern, in southern Sweden.

It’s known for its long lakeside beach, Vätterstranden.

In the center is the 19th-century Sofia Church, with its neo-Gothic design and towering spire.

On the lake island of Visingsö is the Erstad marsh, home to waterbird colonies.
